What is Naturae?

               Naturae a local website was created for three reasons: to inform the people regarding the issues about our environment here locally, nationally and internationally, to create an accesible and open forum made for all ages to understand and participate in, to restore the environment to its natural and healthy state once again. This site was from the minds of four students of Tagum City National High School who with the same advocacy. We are proving that even at our age we can do something for Mother Earth by using our talents and knowledge of technology. This is the integration of the technology man-made and nature. Reminding us even in our own little ways we can help out better the world we live in!

What are our goals?

Inform

Here, in Naturae, we aim to inform people, especially those in our locality, the problems our environment is facing and what we can do to help.

Create

This site is created for accesibility and understandbility that people of all ages are able to use it.

Restore

Naturae aims to restore the environment as it was before and create a clean and sustainable environment maintained by everyone.
