What is Environmental Awareness?

               According to Panchama.org, Environmental awareness is to understand the fragility of our environment and the importance of its protection. Promoting environmental awareness is an easy way to become an environmental steward and participate in creating a brighter future for our children. This is very important in our community since we are a developing city. We need to keep in mind that with the technological advancements, nature will not degrade.

The Activities
